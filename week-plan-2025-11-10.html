Fri Restoration + strength anchor (Priority 5)
  • Option A (if fresh): 25–30 min spin or jog (HR ≤125) to promote blood flow; cap if HR drifts >5 bpm.
  • Strength: Total-body maintenance — keep loads ~70–80% 1RM, RPE ≈7, 2 sets each with 90 s rest: goblet squat 2×10 ↔ dumbbell press 2×8–10 (superset), RDL 2×10 ↔ single-arm row or pull-up 2×8–10, step-up/side 2×10, single-leg calf raise 2×12, optional finisher curls + triceps press-down 2×12 each. Avoid failure; focus on crisp concentric speed.
  • Core: Copenhagen plank 3×20 s + 2×45 s RKC plank to keep trunk tension without extra eccentric load.
  • Breath/mobility: 8 min diaphragmatic breathing + T-spine openers before bed.
  • If week workload accumulates, skip the cardio and keep only strength + breath/mobility work.
