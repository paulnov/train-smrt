Quarterly plan, October 2025

I just came off a successful training for a 50 mile mountain bike race, the VT50. This involved a lot of endurance training and 4-hour rides at 145-155 bpm.

I have a few goals for this fall. I recognize these are a lot of goals and I'm looking for a hybrid plan that makes progress toward all of them.

  1. 5 km PR. I want to hit a sub-20 5k this fall, with about a 6 week training plan.
  1. Be ready for nordic season. This will be a very occasional roller ski, and mainly will be through lifting and core.
  1. Weight training: get back to PRs from earlier in the year.

I plan to put this together by doing two weeks of hard lifting (5-6 workouts per week) from Oct 15-26, with just a bit of running here to maintain. Then on October 26 I start hard-core running workout, with an aim to hit the 5 km PR on ~December 15.

During the running training, I still want to lift 2x per week to maintain my gains.

Schedule constraints

I'm traveling from Oct. 26 -- Nov. 4. I will be able to run regularly (and ride a bit), but weight training will be limited to body weight in hotel rooms. As such, planning to lift every day the week before.

On a given week, I like to schedule 5-6 workouts, but usually i have some busy work days and don't manage to complete all of them. The plan should always indicate which one or two workouts to skip if i have to.
